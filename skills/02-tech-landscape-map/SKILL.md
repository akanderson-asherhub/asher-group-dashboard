# Technology Landscape Map Skill

## Engagement Context

**Positioning:** Recurring quarterly product that keeps clients updated on the proptech ecosystem, emerging threats/opportunities, and competitive moves. Converts to deeper vendor evaluation or strategic roadmap work.

**Pricing:** $1,500–$5,000/quarter (subscription, 4-quarter commitment typical)
**Automation potential:** ~70% automatable via CB Insights, Tracxn, StartUs Insights, Crayon feeds
**Client archetypes:** GC/ENR 400, Owner/REIT, PE/CVC investors, Corporate VC proptech teams
**Conversion potential:** Leads to $5K–$15K Vendor Evaluation Toolkit (when client evaluating a specific tool) or $50K–$200K Market Entry Strategy (for investors or firms entering new markets)

---

## Market Context: Proptech & ConTech Sizing

**Current (2025):**
- Global proptech market: ~$40B
- ConTech (construction technology subset): ~$4.8B at 45% CAGR
- Proptech venture capital deployed: $11.2B in 2025
- Average deal size: $100M+ (mega-rounds dominating)
- Top categories by funding: Real estate tech, construction tech, facilities/property management, workplace

**Projected (2032):**
- Global proptech market: ~$119B (3× growth)
- ConTech: ~$15B+ (at 45% CAGR)
- Capital deployment: Expected to stabilize at $8–$12B/year post-correction

**Trends:**
- AI/ML adoption accelerating (GenAI in design, estimation, safety)
- Consolidation in mid-market (2–5 acquisitions/quarter in contech)
- Investor focus shifting: early-stage (pre-Series A) starved of capital; Series B+ well-capitalized
- Geographic shift: More funding flowing to EU (GDPR compliance + government digitization mandates) and APAC
- Subcontractor tech adoption lagging: opportunity for middle-market integrators

## Categorization Framework: Three Dimensions

### Dimension 1: Project Lifecycle Phase

**Design Phase**
- Architectural and engineering design
- Site analysis and feasibility
- 3D modeling and visualization
- BIM authoring and coordination

**Preconstruction Phase**
- Estimating and bid analysis
- Project planning and scheduling
- Cost control and budgeting
- Supply chain planning

**Construction Phase**
- Project management and collaboration
- Field productivity and mobile tools
- Real-time progress tracking and analytics
- Quality and safety management

**Commissioning Phase**
- Asset handover and documentation
- Equipment startup and training
- Performance validation
- Warranty management

**Operations & Facilities Management**
- Building operations optimization
- Maintenance management
- Energy and sustainability tracking
- Tenant/occupant management

### Dimension 2: Technology Category

**Project Management (PM)**
- Comprehensive project controls (Procore, Viewpoint, CMiC, Autodesk Build)
- Task/team collaboration (Asana, Monday.com, Microsoft Project)
- Document management and EDMS (Bluebeam, Box, Autodesk Docs)

**Estimating & Takeoff**
- Parametric and historical estimating (HCSS, B2W, Sage Estimating, ProEst)
- Takeoff and quantity surveying (PlanSwift, Bluebeam Studio)
- Labor productivity and crew optimization

**Field Productivity & Mobile**
- Mobile job site apps (PlanGrid, Fieldwire, Raken, JMP App)
- Crew time tracking and labor analytics
- Field quality and inspections
- Worker safety and incident management

**BIM/VDC (Virtual Design & Construction)**
- BIM authoring (Revit, ArchiCAD, Tekla Structures)
- BIM coordination and clash detection (Navisworks, Solibri, BIM Collab)
- 3D scheduling and simulation (Synchro, Touchplan, Bridgit)
- As-built capture and digital twins (FARO, Matterport, Reality Capture)

**AI/Analytics & Intelligence**
- Predictive analytics on project health (AlphaSights, Bridgit's Project Intelligence)
- Document understanding and compliance (Buildots, Bridgit, Newmetrix)
- Automated change order detection and claims management
- Generative design and optimization

**Robotics & Automation**
- Autonomous heavy equipment (Autonomous Solutions Inc., Caterpillar self-driving)
- Robotic process automation for admin tasks
- Drone inspection and surveying (DJI, Skycatch, Kespry)
- 3D printing and modular fabrication

**Modular & Prefabrication Management**
- Off-site fabrication scheduling and sequencing
- Modular component tracking and logistics
- Factory controls and quality inspection

**Payments & Finance**
- Lien and payment workflows (Altus Group, Lien.io, Certent Lien)
- Invoice and accounts payable automation (Bill.com, Melio)
- Project-level financial reporting

**Safety & Compliance**
- Safety Culture, Salus Pro, Procore Safety, iSAFE
- Regulatory compliance tracking (OSHA, building codes)
- Incident management and reporting
- Training and certification management

**Workforce Management**
- Labor scheduling and crew optimization
- Skills and competency tracking
- Recruitment and retention tools
- Training delivery platforms

---

### Dimension 3: Maturity Stage

**Emerging (Seed–Series A)**
- Concept validation stage
- Limited market traction (< $1M ARR or < 10 customers)
- High technology or market risk
- Typical funding: $500K–$5M

**Growth (Series B–C)**
- Product-market fit validated
- Scaling sales and product development
- ARR: $1M–$20M
- Expanding customer base and use cases
- Typical funding: $5M–$50M

**Mature (Series D+, Public, or Established)**
- Market leader in category
- ARR: $20M–$500M+ (or profitable without VC)
- Stable customer base, competitive positioning
- May be acquisition target or consolidator
- Public companies (e.g., Autodesk, Salesforce) or well-funded incumbents

## Data Sources & Collection Strategy

### Primary Data Sources (Ranked by Frequency & Reliability)

**CB Insights (Most Comprehensive)**
- 115+ proptech companies tracked
- Funding history, rounds, investors, valuation
- Technology category and use case tagging
- Ecosystem relationships and partnerships
- Update frequency: Weekly
- Cost: ~$50K+/year for enterprise access; free tier available
- How to use: Build custom dashboard by category, pull quarterly snapshots, cross-reference with Tracxn

**Tracxn (Reliable, Cost-Effective)**
- 9M+ companies globally, 25K+ technologies
- 190M+ data points with semantic clustering
- Proptech/contech subset: 500+ key companies
- Funding rounds, team, customer logos, tech stack
- Update frequency: Real-time
- Cost: $2K–$5K/month depending on tier
- How to use: Filter by "Proptech" + "Series B+" for established players; supplement with emerging vendors

**StartUs Insights**
- Semantic clustering of 25K+ technologies
- Proptech-specific reports: annual landscape updates
- Use case mapping (by company size, geography, vertical)
- Market trend analysis
- Cost: Reports $500–$2K; platform access $5K+/month
- How to use: Pull annual market report, validate categorization, identify whitespace

**CB Insights Signals + Tracxn Alerts**
- Monitor competitor technology changes
- Track investor activity (Series A funding spike in safety tech = trend signal)
- M&A activity (acquisitions reveal consolidation pressure)
- Update frequency: Daily/Weekly
- How to use: Scan weekly for major news, flag to client in quarterly update

**Crayon (Competitive Intelligence Automation)**
- Monitors digital footprints of 100+ target vendors
- Auto-generates weekly "Sparks" summaries (product launches, pricing changes, feature releases)
- AI-powered trend detection
- Cost: $5K–$15K/month
- How to use: Curate weekly feeds, surface top 5–10 moves in quarterly report

**Klue (Alternative to Crayon)**
- Unlimited vendor tracking
- AI battlecards (competitive positioning)
- Slack integration for real-time alerts
- Saves 2 days/week in manual monitoring
- Cost: ~$10K–$20K/month
- How to use: Monitor competitor moves, feed into strategic commentary section

**BuiltWorlds Building Tech 50**
- Annual curated list of top 50 proptech vendors
- Includes market leader rankings, emerging categories, hype cycle
- Qualitative: expert commentary from industry leaders
- Cost: Free reports + paid deep dives
- How to use: Validation and positioning context

**CREtech & CRETI Conferences**
- CREtech: Annual proptech summit, 100+ vendors
- CRETI: Commercial Real Estate Technology Initiative reports
- Cost: Conference attendance $1K–$3K; reports $500–$2K
- How to use: Capture new vendors at conference, identify trends, collect vendor one-sheets

**Industry Reports & Press**
- Dodge Construction Network
- Engineering News-Record (ENR) Digital Adoption surveys
- Real Capital Analytics (capital deployment trends)
- PropTechPRO, Built In, TechCrunch Real Estate beats
- Cost: Varies ($500–$2K/year for subscriptions)
- How to use: Supplement with macro trends, validate funding data, spot emerging categories

### Secondary Data (Tactical)

**LinkedIn & Company Websites**
- Team size and composition
- Hiring velocity (signal of funding and growth)
- Customer logos and case studies
- Job postings (R&D investment signal)

**Pitchbook (If Available)**
- Venture database with detailed funding rounds
- Investor networks and syndicate analysis
- Cost: $5K–$20K/month
- How to use: Deep-dive on specific vendors or investor trends
