# Commercial Due Diligence Brief Intake Form

## Engagement: Commercial Due Diligence Brief (PE/CVC)
**Duration:** 2–3 weeks | **Fee Range:** $10K–$25K per deal

---

## Required Fields

| Field | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Target Company Name | text | Company being evaluated for investment |
| PE/CVC Fund Name | text | Your fund name |
| Fund Contact | text | Name, email, phone of deal lead or investment director |
| Deal Stage | select | Preliminary diligence / Active diligence / LOI signed / Other |
| Technology Category | select | Project Management / Workforce Management / Financials / Safety / Equipment / Supply Chain / Facilities / Market Analytics / Other |
| Deal Size (Revenue Range) | text | e.g., "$2M–$5M ARR" or "$100M valuation+" |
| Key Diligence Questions | text | 3–5 specific questions you need answered (e.g., "How sticky is the product? What's churn?") |
| Turnaround Required (days) | number | How many days for deliverable? (Standard: 14–21 days) |

---

## Optional Enrichment Fields

| Field | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Investment Committee Meeting Date | date | When is the IC decision needed? Affects delivery timeline |
| Known Competitors | text | Who are the direct competitors? Helps Asher Group focus competitive intel |
| Target Company Website/Demo | text | Link to product, if available |
| Fund Prior Construction Tech Investments | text | Other companies in your portfolio; helps context |
| Key Concerns | text | Any red flags or specific concerns (e.g., "High churn," "Founder dependent," "Competitive threats") |
| Budget Constraints | text | Is there flexibility in scope/fee? |
| Follow-On Engagement | text | If investment proceeds, will you want ongoing support (retainer, portfolio monitoring)? |

---

## DD Brief Deliverables Include

- **Technology Assessment:** Product maturity, differentiation, technical risks (2–3 pages)
- **Market Validation:** TAM/SAM sizing, growth trends, buyer concentration (3–4 pages)
- **Competitive Positioning:** Competitive matrix, win/loss analysis, positioning (2–3 pages)
- **Team Evaluation:** Founder/management backgrounds, retention risk (1–2 pages)
- **Financial Model Review:** Unit economics (CAC, LTV, churn), projections, sensitivity (2–3 pages)
- **Risk Assessment:** Key risks, mitigation strategies (1–2 pages)
- **Executive Summary:** 2–3 page standalone document for IC distribution
- **Recommendation:** Proceed / Proceed with conditions / Pass

---

## Post-Intake Process

Upon receipt, Asher Group:
1. Confirms turnaround timeline and key diligence questions (24-hour confirmation)
2. Begins research: company website, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, customer interviews (if available)
3. Schedules interview with target company founder/CEO if needed
4. Delivers draft for fund review at day 10; incorporates feedback, finalizes by day 14
