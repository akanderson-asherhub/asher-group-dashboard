# Business Case & ROI Analysis Intake Form

## Engagement: Business Case & ROI Analysis
**Duration:** 4–6 weeks | **Fee Range:** $12K–$25K

---

## Required Fields

| Field | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Company Name | text | Your company |
| Technology Being Evaluated | text | Software/tool name and category (e.g., "Bridgit Bench – Workforce Management") |
| Current Process (Labor Hours/Month) | number | How many hours/month does the current process require? (e.g., 80 hours) |
| Loaded Labor Rate ($/hour) | text | Average fully-loaded rate of people doing this work (e.g., "$75/hour") |
| Expected Efficiency Gain (%) | number | e.g., "30" (meaning 30% fewer hours required post-implementation) |
| Implementation Cost Estimate (USD) | number | Software license + implementation + training (3-year total or 1-year, specify) |
| Implementation Timeline (weeks) | number | How long to deploy and operationalize? |
| Discount Rate (%) | text | For NPV calculation; if unknown, "Use 10% default" |

---

## Optional Enrichment Fields

| Field | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Secondary Benefits | text | Other cost savings, quality improvements, revenue lift (e.g., "Faster delivery = higher client satisfaction = 2% price premium") |
| Risk Factors | text | Anything that could slow adoption or reduce expected benefit? (e.g., "Crew resistance," "Integration complexity") |
| Go-Live Timeline | date | When do you plan to launch? (affects benefit realization timeline) |
| Number of Users | number | Who benefits from the efficiency? (impacts benefit scope) |
| Current Technology Cost | number | What are you currently spending on the process/tool? (helps calculate net TCO) |
| Payback Period Requirement | text | What payback period is acceptable? (e.g., "12 months or less") |

---

## Business Case Deliverables Include

- **Cost Analysis:** Software/implementation/training/change management costs (3-year or 5-year)
- **Benefit Quantification:** Labor savings (hours × loaded rate), quality improvements, revenue lift, cost avoidance
- **Financial Model:** Annual cash flow projections, NPV, IRR, payback period, sensitivity analysis
- **Scenario Planning:** Base case, upside (faster adoption, additional benefits), downside (slower adoption, integration issues)
- **Risk Assessment:** Adoption risks, technical risks, financial risks and mitigation
- **Recommendation:** Go/No-Go with financial justification for leadership
