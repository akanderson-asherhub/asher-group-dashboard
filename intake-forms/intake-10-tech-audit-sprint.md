## Sprint Deliverables Include

- **Tech Stack Inventory (XLSX):** 30–50 systems cataloged with costs, adoption, integration status
- **Gap Analysis Matrix:** Current vs. best-in-class benchmarks by capability
- **Digital Maturity Scorecard (XLSX):** 5 dimensions (Systems Integration, Visibility, Process Automation, Organization, Compliance), 3-point scale, overall gap assessment
- **Prioritized Recommendations (DOCX):** 10–15 actionable recommendations across Quick Wins (0–3 months), Near-term (3–12 months), Strategic (12–36 months)
- **Business Cases (DOCX):** Cost-benefit analysis and ROI scenarios for major initiatives
- **Implementation Roadmap (DOCX/Gantt):** 12–36 month sequencing plan with ownership
- **Executive Report (15–25 pages DOCX):** Professional summary with all findings and recommendations
- **Executive Readout Presentation (10–15 slides PPTX):** Leave-behind deck for leadership circulation

## Sprint Schedule (10 Days)

| Day | Activities | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Kickoff workshop; data collection setup | Meeting notes, interview schedule |
| 2 | Stakeholder interviews (5–8 people) | Interview summaries, refined tech stack |
| 3 | Tech stack inventory consolidation; gap mapping | Tech stack XLSX, gap analysis matrix |
| 4 | Digital maturity scorecard build | Completed 5-dimension scorecard |
| 5 | Recommendations development (Effort/Impact 2x2) | Prioritized recommendation list |
| 6 | Business cases & ROI scenarios | Financial summaries for top 3–5 initiatives |
| 7 | Report drafting (sections 1–6) | Draft report sections, scorecard |
| 8 | Report finalization & scorecard | Final report, scorecard XLSX, 1-page summary |
| 9 | Stakeholder feedback & refinement session | Refined recommendations, confirmed ownership |
| 10 | Executive readout presentation | Final report, scorecard, presentation, signed completion |

---

## Typical Next Steps Post-Sprint

Upon sprint completion, clients typically:
- **Approve Quick Wins** for immediate execution (0–3 months)
- **Budget Near-term initiatives** for vendor selection and implementation planning
- **Engage Asher Group for Technology Roadmap** ($25K–$75K, 8–12 weeks) to develop detailed implementation plans and vendor evaluation
- **Consider Advisory Retainer** ($8K–$15K/month) for ongoing guidance during execution phase

---

## Post-Intake Process

Upon receipt, Asher Group:
1. Confirms sprint timeline and executive sponsor availability (24-hour confirmation call)
2. Issues tech stack inventory template to IT lead (due 3 days before sprint start)
3. Schedules stakeholder interviews (5–8 people across operations, IT, finance, business development)
4. Begins sprint on agreed start date
5. Delivers all findings and recommendations on Day 10 executive readout
