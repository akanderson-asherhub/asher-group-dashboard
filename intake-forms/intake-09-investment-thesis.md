## Investment Thesis Deliverables Include

- **Sector Positioning Document:** Why construction tech, fund fit, competitive VC landscape (30–50 page DOCX)
- **KPMG BuildingTech Taxonomy:** Segmentation across ConTech, PropTech, CRETech, Smart Building
- **Sub-Sector Opportunity Mapping:** TAM, growth rates, competitive intensity, investment stage distribution (with XLSX opportunity matrix)
- **Deal Sourcing Framework:** Screening criteria, scorecard, pipeline targets, sourcing channels
- **Valuation Methodology:** Stage-based pricing, adjustment factors (churn, CAC payback, competition), worked examples
- **Deal Structure & Economics:** Check sizes, ownership targets, term sheet principles
- **Risk Management:** Systematic risks, portfolio-level mitigation strategies
- **Market Data & Trends:** Current funding, growth rates, tailwinds/headwinds, geographic opportunities
- **Investment Committee Deck:** 20–30 slide PPTX for IC and external communications
- **Fund Advisory Board Option:** $25K–$50K/year + potential carry for ongoing portfolio support

---

## Post-Intake Process

Upon receipt, Asher Group:
1. Schedules 1-hour kickoff call with fund leadership to align on thesis hypothesis, key research questions, and decision criteria
2. Conducts 8–10 founder/operator interviews to validate market assumptions
3. Builds valuation model and deal screening framework
4. Delivers draft thesis for fund review at week 6; incorporates feedback, finalizes by week 12
5. Schedules IC presentation and ongoing support arrangements
