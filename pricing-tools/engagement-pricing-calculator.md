## Complexity Multipliers

**Base pricing reflects a "standard" engagement.** For higher complexity, apply multiplier to fee range:

| Complexity Level | Multiplier | Typical Characteristics |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | 1.0× | Single stakeholder base, straightforward decision-making, aligned priorities |
| Complex (1.2–1.4×) | +20–40% | Multi-stakeholder, divergent priorities, organizational politics, integration complexity, prior failed initiatives |
| Enterprise/PE (1.6–2.0×) | +60–100% | Large, highly matrixed organizations; PE portfolio with multiple acquisitions to integrate; high stakes; board-level governance; extensive change management required |

**Examples:**
- Tech Roadmap for 150-person GC (standard) = $25K–$75K
- Tech Roadmap for 500-person multi-regional GC with recent M&A (complex, 1.3×) = $33K–$98K
- Tech Roadmap for PE portfolio company with 4 recently acquired platforms (enterprise, 1.8×) = $45K–$135K

## Negotiation Protocol: "Adjust Scope, Never Rate"

**Pricing Integrity Principle:** Asher Group maintains pricing discipline. If a client negotiates on fee:

1. **Do NOT cut hourly rate or project fee** — this undermines team quality and delivery confidence
2. **Instead, jointly identify scope reductions:**
   - Remove optional deliverables (e.g., "provide summary-only roadmap, skip detailed implementation plan")
   - Reduce interview count (e.g., "6 interviews instead of 12")
   - Narrow focus (e.g., "ConTech only, not PropTech + CRETech")
   - Extend timeline (allows team to work at lower utilization)

3. **Generate revised SOW reflecting reduced scope and matching fee**

**Example negotiation:**
- Client: "Your Tech Roadmap quote is $50K. We only have $35K budget."
- Asher Group: "Here's what we can do at $35K: Current state assessment, gap analysis, top 3–5 prioritized recommendations, and business cases for Quick Wins only. We'll skip the full 3-year implementation roadmap and vendor evaluation scorecard."
- Client approves reduced scope: revised SOW signed at $35K

**Price Floor:** $250/hour minimum or $2,000/day minimum for any engagement
