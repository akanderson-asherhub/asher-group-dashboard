1. **Scope of Work:** This invoice covers only services detailed in the signed SOW. Any out-of-scope work (including client-requested changes, expedited deliverables, or additional analysis) is billed separately at $350–$500/hour.

2. **Acceptance & Completion:** Client acceptance of deliverables is deemed upon receipt and no written objections within 5 business days. This invoice represents completion of the services rendered through the date shown above.

3. **Confidentiality:** All client information, proprietary data, and findings are treated as confidential per the SOW and Asher Group's standard non-disclosure agreement.

4. **Disputes:** Any disputed charges must be reported within 10 days of invoice receipt. After 10 days, the invoice is considered accurate and accepted.

5. **Payment Default:** If payment is not received within 30 days of due date, Asher Group reserves the right to:
